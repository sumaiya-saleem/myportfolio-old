LEPC Website Design
Web Content and Design Intern, Peel Halton Workforce Development Group (LEPC), 2018

The website design portion of my eight week internship at the Peel Halton Workforce Development Group (LEPC). The goal was to develop an interactive website on Experiential Learning, on the company's new project on Experiential Learning in the Peel Halton region, Project ELPH. Along with the web design, my role as a Web Content and Design Intern also consisted of researching for all website content and preparing information packages. All researched content that was also used to populate the final developed site can be viewed here. The following page details on the website design process to create a user-friendly website, including information architecture, and wireframing.

Website Mandate & Rationale
Before beginning high fidelity design and research, we presented the website mandate, goals, and low fidelity information architecture and designs to an advisory group consisting of representatives from Peel and Dufferin-Peel School Boards, City of Mississauga and Brampton, University of Toronto, Sheridan College, Walmart Canada, Education and Multicultural centers, and other employer representatives. The website mandate presented included:

  • Provide a tool thatâ€™s user-friendly, informative and practical that helps to facilitate experiential learning initiatives in peel and halton
  • Communicate the importance and benefits of experiential learning to employers, students and faculty in academic institutions
  • Facilitate efficient planning and delivery of experiential learning programs and initiatives for all stakeholders involved
  • Help grow and support enduring mutually-beneficial relationships built through the experiential learning platform

Information Architecture
For the presentation with the advisory group, we drafted a simple information architecture with only seven tabs. However, after advisory committee meeting, we were advised to keep the website more employer based, and a simple navigation to get to there they are looking for with ease.
